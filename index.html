Swann Story

Swann Planning is a full service agency providing design, planning, and production for events in Washington, D.C. and beyond. Swann Planning services cater to weddings, monumental celebrations, and corporate events.
The belief behind Swann Planning is that an event concept can never be too simple to be transformed into an elegant masterpiece. Placing full attention on every detail, designing an innovative style, and creating a long-lasting memory are key components of a Swann event.
Swann events are special moments for clients, but also for the agency. Swann Planning takes pride in exceeding expectations through premier design, exemplary customer service, and relationships with trusted, highly-regarded vendors.
“Swann Planning strives to positively impact lives not only during the event, but more importantly in the future. I feel truly accomplished when people take time out of their day to reminisce about the amazing time they had at an event Swann Planning coordinated.”
- India Howard, Founder/CEO
